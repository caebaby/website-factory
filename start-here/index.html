The readiness assessment

Six questions. No firm information required.

The questions cover the issues that usually determine whether a transition deserves further investigation. Your answers are not a valuation or recommendation. They simply make the next conversation more useful.

Your final answer determines the next step: schedule a private conversation or receive research that fits your situation.

What we will ask

  • What is making you consider a transition
  • Which business model you are in today
  • When you would realistically consider a change
  • Your approximate trailing-12 revenue
  • How much the practice manages or advises
  • Whether you want to discuss next steps now
