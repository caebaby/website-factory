# Copy Strategy — Mukit Mahdin portfolio
*(Stage 2 · Agent 02. The conversion skeleton adapted for a single-page engineering portfolio. Structure is LOCKED; this maps each skeleton slot to portfolio content.)*

## Big Idea
**"Ships AI-first systems at scale — and can prove the dollar impact."**
Every section exists to overturn the "1+ year = junior" read with evidence of scope, ownership, and outcomes.

## CTA map
- **Primary CTA (everywhere):** `Get in touch` → `mailto:` / #contact
- **Secondary:** `Download résumé`, `GitHub`, `LinkedIn`
- Hero runs both; every section boundary can surface the primary.

## Headline hierarchy
- H1 (hero): positioning line with a **cycling word** on the scale that matters (`systems / pipelines / AI products / features`).
- H2 (sections): plain, confident, no cleverness — "Impact", "Selected work", "How I work", "Recognition".

## Voice
Terse, senior, specific. Numbers with units. Verbs of ownership (architected, designed, built, shipped, delivered). No adjectives from the kill-list. First person, minimal.

## Claims discipline
All numbers are REAL from the CV. Any handle/link not in the CV carries `[VERIFY]`. No testimonials fabricated — omit the testimonial card rather than invent one.
