What we actually do

Four moves that decide the next thirty years.

Each one has its own deadline. Together they decide how much of this windfall actually reaches your family — and your future.

Shelter the gain

QSBS, charitable structures, gifting, and trust timing — executed before the deadlines close, so you keep what the tax code lets you keep.

Diversify the position

A staged, tax-aware plan to unwind concentration without flinching — turning one fragile bet into a portfolio that can't be wiped out by one earnings call.

Structure the estate

Wills, trusts, and entity design coordinated with your attorney so the wealth moves to the people and causes you choose — not to the IRS by default.

Define "enough"

A clear-eyed plan that answers the only question that actually matters: do you ever have to work for money again — and what does this wealth make possible if you don't?
