## Recommended print-safe lines

1. "11,172 experienced advisors changed firms in 2025 — up 16% from the year before and the highest in the four years Diamond Consultants has tracked it." (Diamond Consultants Advisor Transition Report, 2025 data)
2. "Cerulli projects wirehouses will keep shedding advisors at 1.9% a year through 2028, while independent RIAs grow 4% a year." (Cerulli, 2025)
3. "Independent and hybrid RIAs are on track to control nearly a third of intermediary assets — 31.2% — by 2027." (Cerulli, 2023)
4. "In Fidelity's 2023 Advisor Movement Study, 94% of advisors who moved were happy with the decision, and 85% said they gained more control over their future. Independence was the top destination."
5. "In Fidelity's 2018 study, 56% of advisors had considered switching and nearly 1 in 4 did — 64% of movers chose RIA or independent broker-dealer models."

**Caveats:** Diamond Consultants and Cerulli full reports are gated/paywalled — cite the trade-press articles above (WealthManagement.com, Financial Planning, Wealth Solutions Report) or the vendors' own press releases, as linked. Fidelity 2023 stats come from Fidelity's own press release (safe to cite).
