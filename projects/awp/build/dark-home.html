What We Do

One plan. Your CPA and attorney both work from it.

We coordinate your investments, taxes, estate, and business strategy into a single living document — and keep the professionals in your life reading from the same page.

"No one sees the full picture."

Joint planning meetings with your CPA and estate attorney. One living document all three reference. When a tax move is made, your portfolio reflects it — you stop being the translator.

"My portfolio doesn't fit my actual life."

Built around your real constraints — vesting schedules, blackout periods, commodity exposure, concentrated positions — not a model allocation. Managed in coordination with your tax strategy, not in isolation.

"Every bonus season feels like a tax trap."

We sit down with your CPA before year-end — not after. RSU vesting, deferred comp elections, and bonus timing modeled against your real situation. Proactive planning, not reactive prep.

"My RSUs feel like a trap every year."

Vesting-schedule modeling, NQDC elections, 10b5-1 plans, blackout-period strategy. A diversification plan that respects your constraints instead of pretending they don't exist.

"I have real wealth but no plan for after."

We review existing trusts against your current net worth and coordinate with your attorney to fund them correctly and keep beneficiaries current. Legacy planning as a living process, not a one-time document.

"I don't know what I'd walk away with."

We start the exit conversation 3–5 years before the LOI. Entity structure, personal/business separation, pre-sale tax strategy, post-liquidity management. By the time a buyer appears, you're ready.
