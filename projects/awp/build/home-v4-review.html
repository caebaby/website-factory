The work

One plan. Every decision in context.

Services are not separate products. Each one is a lens on the same living plan.

01

Coordinated Planning

“No one sees the full picture.”

Bring the CPA, attorney, and investment work into one shared planning rhythm, so you no longer carry information between the people you hired.

02

Investment Management

“My portfolio does not fit my actual life.”

Build around vesting schedules, concentration, cash needs, and the tax plan instead of treating the portfolio as an isolated account.

03

Tax Strategy & Coordination

“Every bonus season feels like a tax trap.”

Coordinate with your CPA before year-end decisions close, with the planning context already assembled.

04

Equity Compensation

“My RSUs feel like a moving target.”

Model vesting, deferred compensation elections, 10b5-1 considerations, concentration, and blackout timing against the larger plan.

05

Estate & Legacy

“We have documents. We do not have clarity.”

Review how trusts, titles, beneficiaries, and current assets line up, then coordinate questions with your estate attorney.

06

Business Owner Planning

“What would I actually keep if I sold?”

Start the personal planning before the transaction, including valuation context, entity questions, liquidity, and the life that follows.
